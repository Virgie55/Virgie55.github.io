What is CBD?

CBD strains are a bit different because they aren’t a type of cannabis but a cannaboid that is grown from the cannabis. CBD strains can lean both ways, as Indicas and as Sativas. Indicas are known to have a higher CBD to THC ratio when compared to Sativas.

CBD is a cannabinoid derived from the cannabis plant, but unlike THC, can not intoxicate you, but can help with pains and other body stressors. Although not officially proven, CBD strains have been known to help those who suffer with anxiety and PTSD.

Recommended
Strains:

- Charlotte’s Web - one of the best-known high-CBD strains. It contains around 13 percent CBD with little to no THC.

- Lifter is a newer player in the cannabis game. It averages around 16 percent CBD with next to no THC.

- Remedy is a 14 percent CBD strain that produces little to no psychoactive effects.
